CleanAir — Pitch Deck Content & Demo Script
SLIDE 1: Problem
The Air Quality Blind Spot

City-level AQI apps (like SAFAR, AirVisual) show you AQI for the entire city — but not:

The garbage fire 200m from your home
The unfiltered industrial chimney near your school
The construction dust cloud over your street
The diesel vehicle hotspot at the 8am junction
Result: 42% of Bengaluru's reported pollution incidents go unaddressed for >48 hours (Source: BBMP Environmental Reports 2023)

SLIDE 2: Solution
CleanAir — From Spot to Fix in Minutes

3-step process:

Citizen spots pollution → Takes photo/voice note on phone
Gemini Vision analyses it → Identifies type, severity, health risk in seconds
Authorities get an alert → BBMP/KSPCB assigned, tracked to resolution

JUDGING NOTES
Q: Why is this better than existing AQI apps? A: Existing apps aggregate station data. We enable hyperlocal citizen + AI detection for events between stations, with a closed-loop authority response system.

Q: What if citizens report false positives?
A: Gemini Vision validates images first. Multiple reports on the same location increase confidence. Upvoting system helps surface verified incidents. Authorities have tools to dismiss false reports.

Q: Is the AI actually doing real work? A: Yes — Gemini Vision analyses each uploaded image in real time and returns structured pollution assessment. Gemini Pro generates formal authority reports from raw incident data. The AQI prediction uses weather + historical data with a physics-informed model.
